Instructions

Enter your bets in the right-hand column.
For the options where the selected numbers are pre-assigned (short input-field), enter only the number of units to be put at stake.
For the other ones, enter the list of numbers, followed by a slash and the wagered amount.

E.g.: For single numbers: 1/5 (five units on number 1)

For a Street (three numbers on a line) or a Double Street (six numbers on two consecutive lines) only one of the numbers from each of the lines needs to be specified, i.e. 11/5 and 10+11+12/5 both indicate a wager of 5 units on the line containing 10, 11 and 12.

For groups of four (“Corner”, four numbers in a square) all four numbers must be mentioned.

With a group, numbers may be in any sequence, e.g.: a wager of 6 units on the lines containing 16, 17, 18 and 19, 20, 21 can be specified as 16+21/6, or 16+17+18+19+20+21/6 or 21+18/6, etc.
