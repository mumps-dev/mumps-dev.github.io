7.2.1 ARC Draw Command

    drawarc::= expr V ARC , hpos , vpos , radius , startangle , endangle
    radius::= numlit
    startangle::= numlit
    endangle::= numlit

The drawarc drawcommand draws a curved line using the current draw foreground color, draw mode, and pen size. The curved line is the portion of the circumference of a circle, with its center at position hpos,vpos and a radius of radius, that lies between the angles startangle and endangle.
