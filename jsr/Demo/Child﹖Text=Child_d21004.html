Child 210
Bonnie James Campbell
Version D

Cunningham's Songs of Scotland, III, 2, communicated by Mr. Yellowlees.

Narrative

1   High upon Highlands,
and low upon Tay,
Bonnie George Campbell
rode out on a day.
2   'My meadow lies green,
and my corn is unshorn,
My barn is to build,
and my babe is unborn.
