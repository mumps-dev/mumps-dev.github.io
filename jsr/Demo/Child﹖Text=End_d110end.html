C.  C and D were derived from the recitation of Jenny Watson of Lanark and Mrs. Charles of Torry, but which from which we are not distinctly told. An incidental expression of Kinloch's, Manuscripts, VII, 59, may warrant the assigning of C to Mrs. Charles. C is written on the right hand of the Manuscript and D on the left, except that the last two stanzas of D are written on the right, and a few readings of D are written above those of C. (The ink of D is blacker.)
