C.5 Other protocols

The evolution of related protocols may prompt the division of OMI into session-layer and presentation-layer protocols. Some of its functions might then be transferred to other protocols at these levels. The MUMPS Development Committee intends to move this International Standard toward compliance with OSI standards as they emerge.
