*: The attributes FKEYDOWN and FKEYUP are only available in the 1995 ANSI M[UMPS] Application Programmer's Interface standard. In subsequent standards, this attribute has been replaced by a combination of the attributes CHAR and KEYDOWN and KEYUP.
