Calculate π by throwing darts

This program throws darts randomly into the square shown. The value of π is approximated by counting the number of hits within the quarter circle versus the number of hits outside of it. Since the area of the quarter circle is ¼πr2 and the area of the square is r2, the value of π can be approximated as 4*within/total (where within is the number of hits within the quarter circle, and total is the total number of throws).
