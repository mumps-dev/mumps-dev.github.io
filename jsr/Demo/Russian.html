Click on any of the radio-buttons to the left to select a character-set.
Once a character-set is selected, click on any of the key with a yellow label to add a character the text in the above box.
If one of the virtual keyboards is selected, note that the shift key will remain "locked" until clicked again.
Once the text in that box is completed, it can be copied and pasted into any other document.
The physical keyboard can be used to add any characters in the "usual" character-set.
Keys with labels in black are currently non-functional.
